Merge eClinical OS™ On Demand Document Translation powered by Transperfect

Request and receive document translations electronically, within eClinical OS.

With eClinical OS' On Demand Document Translation powered by Transperfect, you can seamlessly request and receive the translation of source and other documents.

Simply click a translation link next to an uploaded document, complete a brief request form, and submit the document. Documents are translated and returned within the study workflow, without the need for a separate contracting or billing process.
